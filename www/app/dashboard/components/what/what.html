"Life moves pretty fast. If you don't stop and look around once in a while, you could miss it."
- Ferris Bueller

Your overall quality of life is guided by how you feel in yourself, and how you feel about your life situation, moment by moment.

Your entire life is just a series of moments, If your mind is preoccupied with thoughts about the future or the past, you are missing the here and now. When we live life through this distracted interpretation of reality, our experience is dulled and you can miss the aliveness of the moment and the fullness of life.

Your ability to go about your day to day life but remain connected with your 'joie de vivre' depends on how you manage the relationship between your mind, body and your attention.

LifeUp helps you maximise your experience of life by providing the education and tools of mind, body and attention management.

The LifeUp approach is based on the following key truisms of interaction between mind, body and attention.

  1. The secret to feeling a deep connection and satisfaction with life is to meet it where it resides, in the moment (not the past or future). This is achieved by managing your mind's disposition to sit in the middle of you and life; to experience life directly rather than as a second hand story as told by your mind

  2. You can't control what your mind thinks but you can identify unhelpful thoughts and choose whether to take them personally or not

  3. Unhelpful emotions are caused by taking unhelpful thoughts personally. You can release an unhelpful emotion by paying attention to the sensation the emotion creates in the body

  4. The trick to changing how you feel, is to first accept how you feel and then to de-personalise the unhelpful thoughts driving the feelings.
