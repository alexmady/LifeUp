Stop drifting, start being
Scott and Jo first happened upon a mutual interest when they met in Barnes in 2012. A lovely part of London with rowing boats, gastro pubs, a village green and a reiki centre!
Jo was hungover, hating her job, single and knocking 30. Scott was regaining balance after a family bereavement, and some health problems.
Both were taking a lot of value from a variety of mindfulness and meditation techniques, by patching together content from weekend courses, books and even Buddhist retreats!
An inspiring goat…
Scott had an idea for making an online mindfulness course to bring peace to the masses (before anyone knew what mindfulness was!). The content was bouncing around in his head from his own experience, he just needed a like-minded, high-energy individual also passionate about mindfulness, to help bring it to life.
Now who could that be?
After many a Sunday afternoon in The Goat, and copious pints of lime and soda (rock ‘n roll), LifeUp was born!
From stressed to best
As they collated the content, developed the course and put their own advice into practice, things started to change in their own lives. In 2013 Jo started a career in Social Enterprise and Scott moved to work part time so he could follow his passion and focus on creating LifeUp.
And guess what else…
Unlimited…
In October 2014 (even after missing the application deadline) Jo and Scott secured funding and one year strategic guidance from UnLtd, an organisation that supports start-up social enterprises.
An overview of our business showed our marketing and branding to be severely lacking...
Enter The Happy Startup School
Mainstreaming Mindfulness
After a well-timed online course with the Happy Start-up School, we matched UnLtd’s funding to enlist the help of the founders in creating our “minimum lovable product”.
LifeUp’s “From Survive to Thrive in 21 days” came to life. Oli, our designer, made some pretty pictures, and we tested, refined, tested and launched!
Mindfulness works for us and it can work for you too… Help us continue the story...
