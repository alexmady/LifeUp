1. LifeUp is a virtual mindfulness training programme to take you from survive to thrive!

  2. The LifeUp app takes you through 6 steps to climb our Mindfulness Mountain – earning 3 badges along the way: Resilience, Connection, Authenticity.

  3. LifeUp 6 steps are acronyms to help you learn simple mindfulness techniques: DISARM, SPACE, FLOW, ACT, BE and I.

  4. Each acronym guides you through a simple process relating to each of the steps: Disarm your thoughts; Create headspace; Find your flow; Act with values; Be in the moment and know yourself.
  1. Look out for the back (<) and forward (>) buttons to navigate through the mindfulness mountain. Note that if you are on the first step you can not navigate backwards – and you cannot navigate forwards if you have not completed all the content at your current level. Swipe left and right to navigate through the content.
  1. On the last slide of each step a tick box will become enabled (top right corner) – check this button to complete the step. Then you will be taken back to the main page to click the ‘CLIMB!' button to proceed to the next step.
  1. Remember, once you have completed the challenge you can go back and review the content at any time.

  2. So good luck with the Mindfulness Mountain 6 steps, and collecting your 3 LifeUp badges along the way!
