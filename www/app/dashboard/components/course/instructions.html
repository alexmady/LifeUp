Instructions
  1. The LifeUp Mindfulness Mountain Challenge is a Mindfulness course which is fully embedded within this app.

  2. The course consists for 6 modules; DISARM, SPACE, FLOW, ACT, BE and I, each of which have a number of slides - the number of letters in the module name corresponds with the number of slides in the module. In fact, each module name is an acronym for the content of that module for example in the ACT module there are three slides; Accept, Connect and Take.

  3. When you first start the course you will be at the DISARM module which is the lowers level of the course and of the midfulness mountain.

  4. On the course page you'll see some buttons like the ones below to navigate through the course content:
  1. When the backwards < and forwards > buttons are enabled you can navigate backwards and forwards through the course modules. These buttons will not always be enabled. If you are on the first module you can not navigate backwards. Also you can not navigate forwards if you have not completed the course content at your current level.

  2. Once you have opened a module by clicking the OPEN 'DISARM' MODULE the module content will be displayed. You will see some dots at the bottom of the module that represent the number of slides in the module.

  3. To go from one slide to the next just swipe your finger across the screen from right to left to move forwards and from left to right to move backwards.

  4. On the last slide of each module a tick button in the top right corner of the screen will become enabled and you can click this button to complete this model. Upon clicking this button you will be taken back to the main course page and you will see a 'CLIMB!' button at the bottom of the screen:
  1. Click the CLIMB! button to climb up to the next level of the course. Once Mike reaches the next level of the course, you can then open the content for that level.

  2. Once you have completed the course you can go back and review any of the content at any level again.

  3. If you have any more questions that are not answered here please feel free to email Alex at alex@lifeup.be
