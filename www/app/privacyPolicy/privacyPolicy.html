Collection Of Information

CREATIVEGOOD SOLUTIONS LTD collects two types of information: Personal information, which means information that can be used to uniquely identify and contact you; and non-personal information, which does not provide direct association with you.

When you register to use LifeUp, we collect the following:


- Your sign-in data (which includes: your name and either email address or Facebook login information), which we use to identify you so you have exclusive access to your account. You can sign in to LifeUp using your Facebook account; in this case, your sign-in is also governed by and subject to Facebook’s privacy policy.


- Your demographic information, which we use for our research and to show you how well you are performing compared to the rest of your demographic. Your demographic information is non-personal.


- Your answers to our motivations questionnaire, which will allow us to create a customised training plan for you. Your answers to our motivations questionnaire are non-personal.


- Device information. We may collect information about the device or any computer you may use to download or stream a copy of the LifeUp application on your device, including, where available, the device’s or computer’s unique device identifiers, operating system, browser type and mobile network information as well as the device’s telephone number, for system administration and to report aggregate information to our advertisers. We may associate device information with your sign-in data and will treat the combined information as personal data in accordance with this policy for as long as it is combined.


- Log information. When you use LifeUp, we may automatically collect and store certain information in server logs, including but not limited to internet protocol (IP) addresses, internet service provider (ISP), clickstream data, browser type and language, viewed and exit pages and date or time stamps.

If you contact us for customer service, we may also collect any information you give us. We use this information to identify you as a customer and help improve our service.

When you use LifeUp, we collect non-personal information on your usage and performance. We use this information to calculate your performance, to provide you with personalised feedback to tell you where you are improving and how quickly, and to construct your training plan (consisting of the goals and games you are served). We also use this information to help us improve the app for future updates.
